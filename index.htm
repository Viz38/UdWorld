About Us

Underground Dance World, Dubai is a unique dance school specialized in teaching dance forms to a varied age group. The uniqueness of this dance academy lies in the fact that it provides training from the ground level and helps the dance enthusiasts to reach the sky. To enroll in the academy, one does not need to have any previous dance experience but they can be assured to turn into confident dancers and competitors, competing in international dance competitions.
