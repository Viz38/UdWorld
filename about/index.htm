About the Art & the Artist

Let's get to know each other

Are you ready to unleash your inner dancer and performer? If yes, then Underground Dance World, Dubai is the perfect place for you.
It is not just a dance school, but a dance community where you can learn, grow, and shine. No matter what your age or skill level is, you will find a course that suits your needs and goals. You will be trained by the best dance choreographers and fitness trainers in the industry, who have worked with many Bollywood stars and won several awards. They will teach you the secrets of various dance styles and fitness programs, from the basics to the advanced levels.
You will also have the chance to show off your talent and compete in international dance competitions. Join Underground Dance World, Dubai today and become a part of the dance revolution.

Meet your trainers

Meet Mr MOHAMMED SHAHEED (Founder and Director), a renowned dance choreographer and fitness trainer who has over 18 years of experience in the industry. He has worked with many Bollywood celebrities such as Salman Khan, Shah Rukh Khan, Prabhu Deva and won several prestigious awards for his outstanding dance skills and creativity. Some of the awards he has received are Arab Asia Starz Season 2 for male dancer 2021, Filmfare Middle East Award for best dancer in 2021, and UAE Achievers Award in 2023.
He is also a social media sensation who has millions of followers on TikTok and Instagram. He loves to share his passion and knowledge of dancing and fitness with his students and clients. He believes that dancing is not only a form of art, but also a way of life that can bring joy, health, and happiness to everyone.

Meet Ms. NIKITHA RAMACHANDRA JOGI, is not just a dancer, but also an educator in the field of dance, with expertise in salsa, semi-classical, and western dance styles. Her work has taken her on a journey across Karnataka, India, enthralling audiences with her live performances.
She has also collaborated with various brands, including PVR Cinemas and Decathlon, showcasing her talent. Her commitment to dance transcends the art form itself, as she advocates for the holistic benefits it can bring to everyone’s life. She firmly believes that dance is more than a hobby or profession; it’s a lifestyle that can infuse joy and positivity into our everyday lives.
